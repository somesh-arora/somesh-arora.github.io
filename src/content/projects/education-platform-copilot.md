## Features Shipped

### Homework Helper, GA Launch
Drove Homework Helper from initial rollout to full General Availability within Copilot's Study & Learn mode. Led cross-functional execution to expand the feature beyond its original scope, shipping an iOS camera mode selector (Photo / Homework) and a new web entry point.

### Proactive Quiz Recommendations
Shipped proactive quiz recommendations powered by AI context signals, generating significant engagement uplift: **2x Quiz DAU** and strong quiz completion rates. This validated the education recommendation strategy and became a reference point for other proactive feature investments.

### Practice Tests
Shipped Practice Tests to production with a disciplined, staged rollout. Monitored performance and quality metrics throughout the ramp to ensure a clean ship with no regressions.

## Impact
Under my leadership, the Education squad drove significant week-over-week engagement growth, validating the AI-powered education strategy and establishing Copilot as a compelling tool for students.
